## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Launching on a weekend** - Weekdays (Tue-Thu) get more traffic
2. **Not responding to comments** - First 2 hours are critical
3. **Being salesy in responses** - Be helpful, not promotional
4. **Ignoring negative feedback** - Address concerns thoughtfully
5. **Not testing links** - Broken links kill conversions
6. **Launching without supporters** - Seed initial upvotes authentically
7. **Writing a boring first comment** - Tell a story, not a feature list
8. **Not engaging with other products** - Community karma matters
9. **Launching too many features at once** - One clear value prop
10. **Not tracking which PH visitors convert** - Add UTM: `?ref=producthunt`
