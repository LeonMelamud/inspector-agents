## üéØ Distribution Strategy

### Key Messaging Themes
1. **Link Magnet**: AI Failures Database (20+ real incidents, fully searchable)
2. **Value Prop**: Free AI testing resources (quiz, checklist, blog)
3. **Credibility**: 500+ failures analyzed, real case studies
4. **Urgency**: These failures are happening now (recent examples)
5. **Actionable**: Immediately useful tools, not just theory

### Platform-Specific Angles

**Product Hunt**: "The Carfax for AI Agents" - validate before you deploy  
**Hacker News**: Technical deep-dive into AI failures database (engineering-focused)  
**Reddit**: Community value-first, no hard selling  
**LinkedIn**: Founder story, mission-driven, professional credibility  
**Twitter/X**: Viral thread format, shocking stats, shareable insights

## üö® Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Don't spam** - Add value first, promote second
2. **Don't cross-post identical content** - Customize for each platform
3. **Don't ignore comments** - Engage within first 2 hours
4. **Don't be salesy** - Lead with problem/solution, not product
5. **Don't forget timezone** - Product Hunt launches at 12:01 AM PT Tuesday
6. **Don't skip proof** - Use real examples, cite sources
7. **Don't oversell** - Be humble, acknowledge limitations
8. **Don't ignore negative feedback** - Respond thoughtfully

## üîÑ Post-Launch Engagement

### Responding to Comments
- **Respond within 2 hours** during launch day
- Thank users for feedback/upvotes
- Address concerns with specific answers
- Link to relevant resources (blog, checklist, quiz)
- Be human, not robotic

### Handling Criticism
- Acknowledge valid points
- Clarify misunderstandings
- Share roadmap for improvements
- Don't get defensive
- Use as learning opportunity

### Amplifying Success
- Screenshot positive comments
- Share on other platforms
- Thank supporters publicly
- Build relationships with engaged users
- Follow up 1 week later with progress update
